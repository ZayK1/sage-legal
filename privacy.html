1. Introduction

Sage ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered skill development platform that helps you create personalized learning plans and track your progress. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Sage, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies, please do not use the app.

2.3 Voice & Speech Data

Important: When you use "Talk to Sage" voice input, your speech is processed using Apple's on-device Speech Recognition. Audio is converted to text locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers as audio. Only the resulting text transcript is sent to our servers for skill plan generation.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve Sage's features
  • AI Plan Generation: To generate personalized learning curricula using AI
  • Progress Tracking: To track and display your learning and habit progress
  • Social Features: To enable skill sharing, comments, and community interactions
  • Communication: To send important updates about your account or the service
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve our app
  • Safety: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or policy violations

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with 12 rounds
  • Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with 7-day expiration
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Database access requires authentication and is rate-limited
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: Data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days after deletion
  • Anonymized Data: We may retain anonymized, aggregated data for analytics indefinitely
